Product 01

Vitamin C Radiance Serum

15% L-Ascorbic Acid · Ferulic Acid · Hyaluronic Acid

Some mornings, you can see the difference before you reach the bathroom mirror. That's what a serum that actually works at the cellular level does — it changes what you see before you've had a chance to form an expectation.

The Lumière Radiance Serum was formulated around a simple principle: Vitamin C only delivers results at concentrations that most brands avoid because they're harder to stabilize. We stabilized it. At 15% L-Ascorbic Acid — the bioavailable form that research has consistently linked to measurable brightening and collagen support — this isn't a token dose. It's the dose.

The formula, explained:

L-Ascorbic Acid (15%) — The gold standard in Vitamin C for skin. At this concentration, studies show it visibly reduces hyperpigmentation, supports collagen synthesis, and neutralizes free radical damage from daily UV exposure. Our nitrogen-sealed amber bottle and anhydrous base keep it active from first drop to last.

Ferulic Acid — The stabilizer that also pulls its weight. Ferulic acid doesn't just preserve the Vitamin C — it doubles its photoprotective efficacy, a finding documented in dermatological research since 2005.

Sodium Hyaluronic Acid (low + high molecular weight) — The smaller molecules penetrate deeper to hydrate at the dermis level, while larger molecules sit at the surface to cushion and plump.

The texture is a light water-gel — fast-absorbing, no sticky residue, no white cast. The faint citrus scent is the Vitamin C itself. There's no added fragrance.

What to expect:

Within 2–4 weeks, most users notice a more even tone and a clarity that doesn't require a filter. By 8 weeks, the difference in photographs is measurable.

Best for: Uneven skin tone, dullness, sun damage, fine lines, and anyone who has tried Vitamin C serums before and been disappointed.

Product 02

Barrier Restore Moisturizer

Ceramide Complex · Bakuchiol 0.5% · Niacinamide 4% · Sea Buckthorn

If your skin is reactive, sensitized, or just perpetually unhappy — the answer is almost always the same. Your skin barrier is compromised. Not permanently. Not dramatically. But enough that everything else you put on your face is either failing to absorb properly or actively irritating you.

The Lumière Barrier Restore Moisturizer was designed for that exact situation: skin that needs genuine repair before it can respond to anything else.

The formula, explained:

Ceramide NP, AP, and EOP Complex — Your skin's barrier is a lipid matrix, and ceramides are its primary structural component — making up roughly 50% of the lipid content in the stratum corneum. This three-ceramide complex mirrors the skin's natural ratio, actively rebuilding what environmental stressors break down.

Bakuchiol (0.5%) — A distinct botanical compound that clinical research has shown to visibly reduce fine lines and improve skin firmness without the peeling, photosensitivity, or dryness of retinoids. A 2019 study in the British Journal of Dermatology found it equivalent to retinol for fine lines and pigmentation with significantly fewer side effects.

Niacinamide (4%) — Visibly minimizes pores, regulates sebum, strengthens the barrier, and brightens uneven tone. At 4%, you get clinical results without the flushing some people experience at higher concentrations.

Sea Buckthorn Berry Extract — Rich in omega-7 fatty acids, beta-carotene, and Vitamin E. We use a highly refined extract so you get the benefits without the vivid orange tint.

Medium-weight cream that melts on contact. Fragrance-free. Dye-free. No essential oils.

What to expect:

Reduction in redness and reactivity within the first week. Skin that was once easily irritated begins to tolerate a wider routine.

Best for: Sensitized skin, compromised barriers, eczema-prone skin, retinol beginners, pregnant and nursing customers.

Product 03

Golden Facial Oil

First-Cold-Pressed Rosehip Seed · Squalane · Marula · Sea Buckthorn

Some products belong in the bathroom cabinet. This one belongs on the nightstand.

The Lumière Golden Facial Oil is an evening ritual — the last step before you sleep, pressed gently between your palms and warmed before it touches your skin. It absorbs quickly but works slowly, through the night, in the way that the best overnight treatments always do: imperceptibly and effectively.

There is nothing in this bottle that doesn't have a reason for being here.

The formula, explained:

First-Cold-Pressed Rosehip Seed Oil — Exceptionally rich in trans-retinoic acid (a natural retinoid) and linoleic acid. One of the few plant oils with genuine, documented evidence for improving fine lines, hyperpigmentation, and skin texture.

Squalane (plant-derived) — Found naturally in your skin's sebum, it absorbs without clogging pores and without that greasy film. Ours is derived from sugarcane, not shark liver.

Marula Oil — High in oleic acid and antioxidants, with a silky, fast-absorbing texture that balances the weight of the rosehip.

Sea Buckthorn CO₂ Extract (trace) — Used at a fraction of a percent, enough to deliver its remarkable omega-7 and carotenoid content without affecting texture.

Warm 3–4 drops in your palms. Press — don't rub — into skin. Let it sit. Go to sleep. In the morning, you'll look like you slept well, even on the nights you didn't.

What to expect:

Within the first week: skin feels more supple in the morning. Within a month: visible reduction in dry texture, improved glow, and noticeable improvement in fine lines.

Best for: Dry and dehydrated skin, fine lines, dullness, and anyone who has earned a proper evening ritual.
