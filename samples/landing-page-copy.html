Frequently asked questions

Still on the fence? Here are the questions we get most often.

Do I need a wearable device (like Apple Watch or Garmin) to use FitTrack Pro?

No — you can log workouts manually and get full access to adaptive planning and analytics. That said, connecting a wearable (we integrate with Apple Watch, Garmin, Whoop, Oura, and Fitbit) does unlock HRV-based recovery scoring, which makes the AI planning significantly more accurate. If you have a device, connect it. If not, FitTrack Pro still works great.

I'm a beginner. Is FitTrack Pro overwhelming to use?

We designed it for beginners and advanced athletes alike. When you sign up, you tell us your current fitness level, goals, and equipment access — and the AI builds a plan matched to where you actually are. The app hides advanced features until you're ready for them. Most beginners are logging their first workout within 10 minutes of signing up.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your workout logs and progress data stay on file for 12 months after cancellation. You can export everything to CSV at any time — your training history belongs to you. If you resubscribe within 12 months, everything picks up exactly where you left off.

How is FitTrack Pro different from MyFitnessPal, Strava, or other fitness apps?

Most apps record what you do — FitTrack Pro tells you what to do next. MyFitnessPal is primarily a food tracker. Strava is a social activity log. FitTrack Pro is a training intelligence platform: it analyzes your performance, recovery, and goals, then adapts your training plan dynamically. Think of it as the difference between a food diary and a nutritionist.

How do the 1-on-1 coaching sessions work on Pro+?

When you upgrade to Pro+, you're matched with a certified trainer from our vetted coaching team based on your goals and sport. Sessions are 30 minutes via video call, scheduled through the app. Your coach gets full read access to your FitTrack Pro data before each session — so you spend the call on strategy, not catching them up. Two sessions are included per month; additional sessions can be booked at $35 each.
