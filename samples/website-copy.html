About Us
About Clearpath Advisory

We started Clearpath because good accounting shouldn't feel like a black box.

Most accounting firms are organized around efficiency — serving as many clients as possible, as quickly as possible. Nothing wrong with that. But it means clients rarely understand what's being done on their behalf, and rarely feel confident making business decisions with the financial information they receive.

How Clearpath Started

In 2012, our founder Catherine Marsh left a Big Four firm with a simple conviction: small and mid-sized businesses deserve the same quality of financial thinking that large corporations get — delivered in a way that actually makes sense to the people running the business.

She'd spent 8 years in corporate tax and advisory work, watching brilliant entrepreneurs make avoidable financial mistakes because their advisors spoke in a dialect nobody else understood. Jargon as a moat. Complexity as a feature. Reports that explained what happened without explaining what to do about it.

Clearpath was built around a different model: fewer clients, deeper relationships, and a commitment to leaving every client more financially literate than when they arrived. Over twelve years, that model has attracted 200+ clients who stay — our retention rate sits above 97% — because they feel genuinely supported, not just serviced.

Today, Clearpath has grown to a team of seven CPAs and financial advisors. We're still boutique by design. We take on a limited number of new clients each quarter so we can continue to do our best work for the ones we have.

What We Believe

Clarity is a service.

A financial report that confuses you is a failed financial report, regardless of how technically correct it is. We've made a commitment to explain everything in the language you actually think in.

Relationships over volume.

We deliberately limit how many new clients we take on each quarter. If we can't give you our best work, we'd rather refer you to someone who can. Our reputation is built on retention, not growth at all costs.

Proactive beats reactive.

The best tax planning happens in January, not April. The best cash flow conversation happens before the crunch, not during it. We check in before you need to call us.

No surprises.

Fixed-fee engagements on every service. You know exactly what you're paying before we start. No scope creep, no invoice surprises. Financial advice shouldn't create financial anxiety.
